A modern OLED panel layers a sheet of organic compounds between two electrodes. Each subpixel emits its own light, which is why blacks are truly black and the panel can switch off entire regions to save power. There are no backlights anywhere in this slab.

Built as a single self-contained HTML page. No frameworks. Component scale and arrangement are stylised — real boards are denser than this.
